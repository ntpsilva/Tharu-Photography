About Us

Welcome to Tharu Photography, where we transform moments into timeless memories. Founded in 2010, we've been dedicated to capturing the essence of life's most precious occasions with creativity and passion.

Our team of professional photographers brings a unique artistic vision to every project, whether it's a wedding, portrait session, or commercial assignment. We believe in authentic storytelling through imagery that speaks to the heart.

With state-of-the-art equipment and a keen eye for detail, we ensure every shot is perfect. Our approach combines technical expertise with a personal touch, making every session comfortable and enjoyable.
